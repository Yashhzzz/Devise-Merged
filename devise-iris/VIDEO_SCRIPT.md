# 🎬 Devise — 3-Minute Demo Video Script

> **Total Runtime:** ~3 minutes  
> **Format:** Screen recording of the dashboard + voiceover  
> **Tone:** Confident, clear, slightly punchy — like a YC demo day pitch

---

## 📍 INTRO [0:00 – 0:20]

> *[Show the landing page or a blank screen with soft background music]*

**VOICEOVER:**

> "Every company is using AI tools. ChatGPT, Claude, deepseek, Gemini, Midjourney — your employees are already on them.
> But here's the problem: **IT has no idea what's happening.**
> No visibility. No control. No compliance.
> That's what **Devise** fixes."

---

## 📍 THE PROBLEM [0:20 – 0:45]

> *[Cut to a simple split-screen or just keep the landing page]*

**VOICEOVER:**

> "Right now, when an engineer pastes your source code into an AI chatbot — you don't know.
> When a sales rep uploads a client contract to an AI tool — you don't know.
> When someone starts using an unapproved, high-risk AI API — **you. don't. know.**
>
> Shadow AI is the new Shadow IT. And it's growing fast."

---

## 📍 THE SOLUTION — DASHBOARD WALKTHROUGH [0:45 – 2:20]

> *[Open the dashboard at localhost:8081, navigate slowly and deliberately]*

**VOICEOVER:**

> "Devise is an AI activity monitoring platform that gives your IT and security teams real-time visibility into every AI tool being used across your organization."

---

### 🔹 Live Feed [0:50]
> *[Click on Live Feed tab — show the event list]*

> "This is the **Live Feed**. Every time someone on your team connects to an AI tool — ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, any of them — it shows up here in real time.
> You can see **who** used it, **what device** they were on, **what process** was running, and the **risk level**."

---

### 🔹 Alerts [1:05]
> *[Click on Alerts tab — show the high-risk events]*

> "High-risk activity gets flagged immediately. **Unapproved tools. High-frequency API calls. Binary tampering.** Your team gets alerted before it becomes a compliance incident."

---

### 🔹 Analytics [1:20]
> *[Click on Analytics tab — show charts]*

> "The **Analytics** tab gives you the big picture. Which tools are being used most? Which departments are the heaviest users? What time of day is AI usage spiking?
> This data turns anecdotal concerns into boardroom-ready reports."

---

### 🔹 Devices [1:35]
> *[Click on Devices tab]*

> "The **Devices** view shows every machine running the Devise agent — our tiny background process that runs silently on employee laptops. You can see which devices are online, their OS, and the last time activity was detected."

---

### 🔹 AI Firewall [1:48]
> *[Click on Firewall tab]*

> "And here's where it gets powerful — the **AI Firewall**. You can **allow or block** specific AI tools across your entire organization with one click. Block Replicate. Allow GitHub Copilot. Your policy, enforced automatically."

---

### 🔹 Budget / Subscriptions [2:02]
> *[Click on Budget/Spend tab]*

> "Finally — **spend visibility**. See which AI subscriptions are active, which ones are zombie licenses nobody is using, and whether you're over budget. On average, companies waste **30% of their AI spend** on unused seats."

---

## 📍 HOW IT WORKS [2:20 – 2:40]

> *[Stay on dashboard or show a simple architecture diagram if you have one]*

**VOICEOVER:**

> "Here's how it works. Devise has two parts:
>
> **One** — a lightweight Python agent called **Devise Eye**, installed on employee machines. It monitors outbound network connections, identifies AI tools by their domains, and streams that data to the cloud.
>
> **Two** — this dashboard, **Devise Iris**, where your IT and security team gets a complete, real-time picture of everything."

---

## 📍 CLOSING [2:40 – 3:00]

> *[Return to the Live Feed or the main dashboard stats view]*

**VOICEOVER:**

> "AI adoption isn't slowing down. But unmonitored AI usage is a security risk, a compliance liability, and a budget black hole.
>
> **Devise** gives you control — without slowing anyone down.
>
> If your company is serious about AI governance, we'd love to show you a live demo."

> *[Fade out with product name / tagline on screen]*
> **"Devise — The Firewall for the AI Era."**
